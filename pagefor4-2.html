Makeup Brand Project (SEVEN)


Production period: Oct.10th.2022 - Nov.11th.2022


It is a project to produce logos, labels, cosmetics, magazines on two pages,
and banners in nine sizes using the brand Seven.

To set the concept of the brand's design,
I planned the target age of the brand and the value that the brand wants to pursue.


The main logo was expressed artistically using the shape of the number '7'.
Seven is a cosmetics company that exists for young friends and people with dreams and wishes.
The main age group is between teens and early 20s.
I designed a magazine by comparing our dreams and wishes to clouds floating in the sky
that we had at least once in our childhood.


It can be seen that the pink clouds in
the magazine were expressed using pink, the main color of our brand.
