Beer Brand Project (Muskoka)


Production period: Feb.3rd.2021 - Mar.30th.2021


IIt was a project to recreate the beer brand with the logo design of another brand.
The logo shown below was designed by combining the
characteristics of the brand logo of the cleaning product called
windex and the text of the brand of beer called Muskoka.

About logo

The biggest feature of Windex's logo is that it becomes sleek toward the end of the log on the bold type.
I tried to redesign Muskoka using these two features.
The sleek shape of the windex logo was intended to be expressed
as the coolness felt when drinking beer.

Another logo tried to briefly include information about Muskoka.
In addition, detailed elements were added to prevent the logo from looking bored.

About packaging

The packaging was designed to allow you to carry
around three beer bottles to suit Muskoka's slogan, which can be drunk anytime, anywhere.

In addition, beer bubbles were expressed as illustrators
on the mobile version page to express the image of beer
